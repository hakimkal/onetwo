About Us

About 1212 Polo

12-12 Polo and Turf club is the first of its kind in Abuja. established with the aim of bringing the beautiful game of Polo and the exhilaration of horse racing to thrill seekers in the booming metropolis of the FCT.

Conceived in the mind of the club founder as a tribute to the establishmet of Abuja as a capital which the official inauguration took place on the 12th of December 1991; The club took its name from this inauguration date.

The name was made synonymous to Abuja's inauguration date to allow the club epitomize the strength, beauty and resourcefulness of the FCT.
