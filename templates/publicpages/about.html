About 1212 Polo

12-12 Polo and Turf club is the first of its kind in Abuja established with the aim of bringing the beautiful game of Polo and the exhilaration of horse racing to thrill seekers in the blooming metropolis of Abuja the Federal Capital of Nigeria (FCT).
The 12-12 Club was conceived in the mind of the club founder as a tribute to the establishment of Abuja as a capital. The official inauguration of the FCT took place on 12th of December, 1991; the club took its name from this inauguration date to allow the club epitomise the strength, beauty and resourcefulness of the FCT

Mission & Vision

To contribute and take the game of Polo and Horse racing to newer altitude in Nigeria.

To be the benchmark in Horse racing and Polo sports in the country. 12-12 is aligned with the aspiration of Abuja as a city, which is to be the greatest capital in Africa.
